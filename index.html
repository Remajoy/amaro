Welcome to SDG 5

SDG 5 focuses on achieving gender equality and empowering women worldwide. This goal aims to eliminate discrimination, prevent violence, ensure equal opportunities, and create an inclusive future for all. By understanding history, exploring progress, and raising awareness, we pave the way toward real change.

About This Website

The Progress Scroll: Gender Milestones is an interactive website that highlights historical achievements in the fight for gender equality using a long-scrolling design. As users move through the page, JavaScript reveals key events, challenges, and breakthroughs through smooth animations and interactive visuals. This project helps students and learners understand the evolution of gender rights and encourages awareness and advocacy for SDG 5.

Why Gender Equality Matters

  • It improves economic growth and development.
  • It creates safer and more just societies.
  • It ensures equal access to education, work, and rights.
  • It protects women and girls from violence and discrimination.
  • It promotes strong and healthy communities worldwide.
