About Jaro

Jaro is one of the oldest and most historically significant districts of Iloilo City. Known for its grand Spanish colonial houses, stunning churches, and rich cultural heritage, it was once a separate city before being incorporated into Iloilo.

The district is famous for the **Jaro Metropolitan Cathedral** and the **Jaro Belfry**, both standing as powerful symbols of faith and resilience. During the Spanish colonial era, Jaro was considered a center of religion, education, and commerce in the Visayas.
